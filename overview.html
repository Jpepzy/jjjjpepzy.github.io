Overview

Ours is a child day care centre. Our main aim is to always give out the best. To us, children are peas in a pod, and they always come first.
Each child is his or her own individual. So then, we take care of each differently.

We believe every child is individually special and is cared for as such.
We have qualified and experienced staff at hand to attend to your childrien's needs. We Committed to providing 1st class care and training in a safe and friendly environment.
We have books, toys, and equipment to keep your children occupied all day.
This centre offers extra curricula activities such as swimming and piano lessens for pre-schoolers.
The centre is committed to bringing out the best in each day.

Enrolment at this centre is limited and admission is in progress.


a) Our daily programme is to ensure each child is brought in happy to start
his/her day at the Centre.
(b) To make sure they are well fed.

(c) To make sure they are well looked after.
(d) To make sure all their needs are met to the best of our ability.
(e) To make sure we have a good team between the parents, us and the child.
(f) To have a good relationship between the parents and us.
(g) To share our feelings, problems, about each child.
Children are not allowed to bring any medication to school unless specified by a doctor.
(b) We need to know if a child is on any medication.
(c) We need to know if a child has an allergy.
(d) Keep your child at home if he/she
. Has a fever or has had one during the previous 24 hour period;
. Is taking an antibiotic;
. Has a cold that is less than four days old;
. Has a heavy nasal discharge;
. Has a cough;
. Is fussy, cranky, tired and/or generally not himself/herself;
. Has diarrhea;
. Has been vomiting;
. Has sore throat.
(e) In case of accidental injury, we will make an immediate attempt to contact a parent.
If we cannot reach you, we will call the child’s doctor. If necessary,
we will take the child to the hospital. Your signed consent form will allow the caregiver in charge to make necessary decisions about the care of the child.
(f) It is to your child’s benefit that you keep us up to date on phone numbers, emergency numbers and other pertinent information.

STAFF / PARENT MEETING


(a) There is a staff / parent meeting once every two months.
(b) Should a parent want any help from us regarding the welfare of a child, our school etc; the parent is always welcome to come to the office anytime.

UNIFORM

(a) Girls aged between 12mths – 24mths should wear red checkered dress in the prescribed school design.
(b) Girls aged between 24mths – 60mths i.e. 2yrs – 5yrs should wear blue checkered dress in the prescribed school design or grey pleated skirt with white or blue polo blouse.
(c) Boys aged between 12mths – 30mths should wear grey trousers with white polo shirt with the school logo.
(d) Boys aged between 30mths – 60mths i.e. 2 ½ yrs – 5yrs should wear grey trousers with blue polo shirt with the school logo.
(e) Children aged between 9mths to 12mths are allowed to wear comfortable loose clothing to school
(f) Children’s shoes should be very comfortable to walk and play with.
(g) If your child is under 2 years, please ensure additional clothing and diapers are added. Three should be enough.
(h) If your child is under 3 years and is not potty trained, please bring in additional clothing and diapers. Two should be enough per day.

TOYS


(a) Except for toys that are needed during your child’s first days to ease the transition from home, we ask that you leave your child’s toys at home or in the car unless of course, your child is willing to share them with everyone.
(b) We do not accept any responsibility for damages to toys brought in by children.
(c) Some toys are not allowed on the premises e.g.: gun toys, war toys and toys that are not safe. If in doubt please ask one of the caregivers.

FEES:

(a) Fees are to be paid monthly/termly. It has to be paid before the beginning of each month or term. Fees once paid are not refundable.
(b) If a parent has difficulty in paying fees at a particular point in time, we need to know to arrange a payment plan for the person.
(c) If a parent has more than one child in the centre, there is a discount of 5% off their total fees.
: Please do not hesitate to let us know of any problems concerning your child.

COMPLAINTS

If you are not happy with the service we provide or have a complaint about a member of staff, you may write to us and we will investigate and try to resolve the matter within a week.
Once again we would like to welcome both parents and children to our day care centre and hope that your time spent with us is both enjoyable and educational.
